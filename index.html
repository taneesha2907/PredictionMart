What is a Prediction Market?

In a prediction market, you can buy and sell “shares” tied to specific events. The price of a share reflects the market’s belief in the probability of that event happening.

For example, if “Will SpaceX land on Mars by 2030?” trades at $0.22, the market thinks there’s a 22% chance of it happening.

How It Works

  1. Sign Up: Create your account and verify your identity.
  2. Deposit Funds: Add money to your wallet securely.
  3. Trade: Buy or sell “Yes” or “No” shares for future events.
  4. Settle: When outcomes are known, payouts are made automatically.

Example Transactions

Example 1: You buy 100 shares of “Will Apple release an AI iPhone by June 2025?” at $0.40 per share. If it happens, you earn $100 - $40 = $60 profit.

Example 2: You sell “Yes” shares for “Will global temperatures exceed 1.5°C in 2024?” at $0.70. If it doesn’t happen, you keep the $0.70 per share as profit.
